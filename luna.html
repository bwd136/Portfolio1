Project Name: My Civic Hatchback
Year: May 2019

 

 

My First Car
My first car I was getting was a current generation Civic Hatchback which the Civic Type R is based on. I was so excited that I got a car and knowing that I had a similar drawing of the car already, I used that as a base to draw it. Luna is my car's name and comes from the colour of the car, Lunar metallic Silver.

 

I made the drawing a dream version of what I would like my car to look like. I kept it realistic to parts that I could potentially afford and install on my car. I added little aero bits, lowered the car and put some wheels that I think would look good.
