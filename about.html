Hey there, I'm Brendan!

I am a Toronto based visual designer with a passion for creating digital platforms and tools that will enable people to elevate their lives. I choose to become a designer as I am able to be a translator of digital technology for problems.

Outside of design, I am a passionate car enthusiast and love to involve myself with cars as much as I can creatively. I love to sim time attack, illustrate, and 3d model/render cars.

I am currently studying Global Business and Digital Arts at the University of Waterloo, pursuing my bachelors degree.

Feel free to reach out at brendanwong20@gmail.com and connect with me on social media. I’m always down for a nice conversation!
