Project Name: Dream Car
Year: April 2019

 

 

The First One
My first attempt at creating vector illustration that took more than 2 weeks of percerverious to achieve the final result. It was one night, around 3 am, I told myself to keep going and eventually it started clicking with the shading. Being able to breakthrough in car graphics allowed my visions come to life.

 

This is a Toyota 86, also my dream car, and this is the vision of how I would like to build one in real life. The car is full of Japanese tuner parts and built to be a streetable track car. The parts on the car is to blend function with form.
