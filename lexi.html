Project Name: Lexus IS300
Year: June 2019

 

 

Could of Been My First Car
This old Lexus IS 300 was the car I was expecting to get as a first car but plans change. I still have a soft spot for these car and would still like to own one some day. This drawing was for me to experiment with the shading to make the colour changing more modular and simple. I was using the gradient tool instead of making the gradient lines myself.

 

The car is a grey colour as one of my favourite race car drivers owns one in grey. I then added a Japanese popular wheel that was discontinuing as I kept seeing people having them on social media. The white colour for the wheels is to contrast the darker colour of the body. I wanted to keep the car looking from the factory with a bit of Japanese tuning influences.
