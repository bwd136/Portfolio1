Car Illustration Process

  • Illustration
Tools:

Adobe Illustrator

 

Date: April 2019 (2 weeks)

Project Type: Personal

 

Check Out my Instagram for More Car Content:

@why_tofu

 

Overview:

Drawing vector car illustrations are my side projects. Cars are one of my passion and virtually modifying them is satisfying to me. Drawing helps me visualize car parts I would like to see on a stock car that I wonder about in my mind. I would want to own the cars I draw one day and creating them helps me live a part of that dream.

 

References:

Steps using Adobe Illustrator:

1. Find reference images for body and wheels
2. Create a colour palette of 7 changing the brightness
3. Outline the body and stroke
4. Add interior details and windows
5. Add highlights, shadows, exterior details
6. Add brakes, tires, wheels of choice

 

 

Biggest Challenges:

Whenever I digitally illustrate the cars, the longest process is figuring out the highlights and shadows. This takes a lot of trial and error which comes with practice and learning more about reflective surfaces.

 

Takeaway:

I still have a long way to go in terms of learning shadows and highlights and this is just the beginning. The motivation to continue drawing these cars is the ability to put car ideas from the mind onto a visual medium and inspire others.
